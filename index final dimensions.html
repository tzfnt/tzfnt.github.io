TIZIANO FANTAPPIÈ

Composer, Producer, Multi instrumentalist

Bio

Born in Firenze in 1990, he begins playing guitar at twelve years old. He fell in love with music thanks to Simon & Garfunkel, Led Zeppelin, Frank Zappa and Miles Davis. Fantappiè then started played guitar in bands mainly playing original music, until in 2017 he graduates at the Conservatorio L. Cherubini di Firenze in the Jazz department, with a 20 minutes suite degree of original music, made through the concept of Palle Mikkelborg of mixing and knocking down musical genres. Following this path, with his band Damistrok Zane he made his first album which was released in 2022 for aSpaceship Records, featuring artists like Cosimo Boni, Giacomo Petrucci, Iacopo Perra.
In 2014 he begun working as a composer and one of the first works was the show Pindarico of TerzoStudio, thanks to the collaboration with the theater company MascheraViva.
Fantappiè actually lives in Madrid, developing his music projects as well as composing for advertisements, movies, video games, theater.

Style & Works

Tiziano Fantappiè is fascinated by every aspect related to music, from composing, arranging and producing up to live performances in bands. His use of real instruments - often handmade - mixed with MIDI computer sounds and pedal effects makes his style multicolored and experimental.
His latest works include music for advertisements of Dolce & Gabbana, Galleria Nazionale delle Marche, Zucchetti, soundtracks for documentaries, animation, theater shows, videoclips and videogames, as well as writing and producing for bands.
